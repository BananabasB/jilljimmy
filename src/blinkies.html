About our blinkies!

All blinkies/badges on this page come from blinkies.cafe, a site you can make your own blinkies on. All artistic credits go to them.
If you're looking into adding our blinkie to your website, first of all, thanks! That's really kind of you, even though we do not want our work affiliated with any kind of inappropriate content. If your site contains that stuff, we'd prefer it if you didn't put our blinkie there, thanks!
