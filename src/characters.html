Characters

Jill Jimmy is full of loads of interesting characters with cool backstories. Here you can learn about all of these cool characters and sick sidekicks. What are you waiting for? Let's go!

Bagel

Bagel is a worm who is Jill Jimmy's best friend and trustworthy assistant. He can do just about anything!

moBot

moBot is a cool robot with jet boosters and built-in intelligence. In a split second, it can perform complex calculations and assist in ways no human can.

Jill Jimmy

Jill Jimmy is the main character with the main character energy. He's always ready to tackle things head-on and had a can-do attitude.
